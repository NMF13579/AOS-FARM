# AOS-FARM.456 — Forbidden Behavior Evidence Mapping Concept

Forbidden behaviors must no longer remain bare declarations. They must be explicitly mapped to evidence to guarantee that no destructive or unauthorized actions occur.

The mapping flows as follows:

`forbidden_behavior`
→ `evidence item`
→ `verification method`
→ `status`
→ `Human Review note`

This structured mapping ensures that automated validators can verify the *existence* of the mapping, while humans can explicitly verify the *semantic correctness* and authorize the execution.
